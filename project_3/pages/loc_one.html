LaFayette is a small town located
in the the upstate New York area
and has been considered as a
true rural area. I was born
here and lived here for most of my life up
until about 10 years old. Living in such
emptiness was hard especially for a
kid, it felt almost impossible
to find something to do
when the closest stores were
five miles away.
